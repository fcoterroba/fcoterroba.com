It may be normal that you don‚Äôt know what the DNI is since you might be reading me from the other side of the pond and have no idea what this is.

I‚Äôm not an expert in legality but the DNI is the identity document issued in Spain. Since March 2006 it has been electronic. It‚Äôs a polycarbonate card that incorporates a chip with digital information, which has dimensions identical to commonly used credit cards. It‚Äôs mandatory from the age of 14, although it can be requested from the minor‚Äôs registration in the Civil Registry. As of October 2015, more than 44 million electronic DNIs had been issued in Spain.

Basically, without Wikipedia, it‚Äôs a mandatory identity card used in Spain.
