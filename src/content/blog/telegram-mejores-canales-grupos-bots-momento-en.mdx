**BOTS**

A Telegram bot is a very old functionality, it's more than 3 years old and doesn't have a common explanation since each bot is different and does very different functions from each other.

But it could be summarized by saying that bots are small programs within the same program.

Most of them are in English, but it's very simple. To add it you have to follow the link I leave you below or search for them with @.

1. [@fbvideo_bot](https://t.me/fbvideo_bot). Don't be fooled by its name, this bot is capable of downloading videos from Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, imgurl and even reddit using only the link. In 3 seconds it sends you the file so you can download it.

2. [@converto_bot](https://t.me/converto_bot) is a bot that allows you to download mp3 and mp4 from any YouTube video. You simply have to type mp3 or mp4 followed by the link and in less than 1 minute you'll have the link to download it directly.

![Converto Bot](https://fcoterroba.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/5.png)

3. [@TexttSbot](https://telegram.me/texttsbot) is a bot that allows you to create loquendo voices (computer-generated) in almost any language and without any limit. I don't use it daily but it's funny ðŸ˜‚

4. [@Watch_Bot](https://t.me/watch_bot) is a simple bot that limits itself to constantly observe your website and instantly alerts you of possible crashes. In case you want to say goodbye to hosting a bit ðŸ˜‚

5. If you want to have a fun time, [@Gamee](https://telegram.me/gamee) is your best bot. It's an online minigame page that this bot has created where you can play an infinity of games without leaving Telegram or installing programs.

6. If you're a cryptocurrency machine, [CryptoWhaleBot](https://t.me/CryptoWhaleBot) is your bot. It's a simple bot that gives you the current value of most cryptocurrencies. As well as its balance in 1 hour, 1 day and 1 week. In addition to showing the value of the requested currency in Bitcoins, in case you wanted to do Exchange.

7. [@RaeBot](https://telegram.me/RaeBot) is an UNOFFICIAL bot to consult word definitions according to the RAE, the body that governs Spanish linguistics. Its use is simple, you just have to type @raebot followed by the word you want to see definition. (You can use it in any chat)
